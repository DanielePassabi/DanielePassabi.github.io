ABOUT ME

I have always been interested in the world of IT, ever since I was a child.

I started my university career by majoring in Internet of Things, Big Data & Web. During this Bachelor's degree, I became particularly passionate about the world of Machine Learning and Data Analysis.

For this reason, I then attended and completed a Master in Data Science at the University of Trento.

I started my career as a Data Scientist at HPA | High Performance Analytics. My role mainly focused on data analysis and the development of artificial intelligence solutions, particularly in the field of Natural Language Processing, for which I am in charge.

As my career evolved, my role within the company did too. I transitioned from the position of Data Scientist to that of Chief Operating Officer (COO). In this leadership role, I am able to leverage my technical background to guide our team towards achieving the company's goals.
